The Best Movies According to Sir Dozie

My best 3 movies of all-time.

This is a movie that covers a lot of the Igbo people, their culture and their way of life before the arrival of the British.
It was first written as a novel by Chinua Achebe who also is one of my best Igbo poet.

Ikuku

This is an Igbo movie about the entities of one of the four energies in Igbo methology.
